Conscious or not, welcome!

My main research interest is consciousness, and whatever can help me understand it better. More concretely, for the foreseeable future, I am trying to see whether ideas from structural causaal models (SCMs) and causal inference can help clarify, compare and test the plethora of theories of consciousness out there. On this website you can find book reviews, assessments of consciousness theories, and my topical "humour" that I couldn't keep to myself.

Book reviews

Summaries and impressions of science-oriented books. In progress: my take on Theory and Reality by Peter Godfrey-Smith (2nd ed.).

Consciousness theories

Summaries, comparisons, and critiques of theories of consciousness. I try to cast theories in more graphical terms via SCMs hoping to extract some insights from doing so.

Attempted humour

A running thread of humourous and odd ideas and observations, packaged as short blog posts. Tongue-in-cheek. Updated as inspiration strikes.
