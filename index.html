Conscious or not, welcome!

My main research interest is consciousness, and whatever can help me understand it. More concretely, for the foreseeable future, I am trying to see whether ideas from structural causal modelling (SCMs) and causal inference can help in clarifying, comparing and testing the plethora of theories of consciousness out there. On this website you can find my book reviews, assessments of consciousness theories, publications, and topical "humour" that I couldn't keep to myself.
